Meadow House Floral Design is a local studio dedicated to serving real-life couples looking to bring beauty into life’s most special moments.

Each design has one goal: to touch the heart of the recipient
in a way that shows them they are known, seen, and deeply loved.

Hi there! My name is Meagan, the gal behind the blooms at Meadow House. From start to finish, I will take your ideas and turn them into a beautiful, flowering reality that will inspire moments of rest, appreciation, and wonder.
